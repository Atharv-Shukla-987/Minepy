A minecraft clone made in python language using a library called ursina {not fully developed yet , in its initial pharas} . It spawns you in a plain world . You can only mine blocks and place blocks yet . It spawns tree as you move , it has good textures and sound effects .

# CONTROLS

Press w ----> for moving forward
Press S ----> for moving backward
Press D ----> for moving right
Press A ----> for moving left
Press Space bar ----> for jump
Mouse Right click----> placing blocks
Mouse left click ----> mining blocks
Press Shift --------> for crouch
